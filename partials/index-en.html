Frequently Asked Questions

What is an interactive projection system?
An interactive projection system combines a projector with motion sensors. It projects images onto floors, walls or other surfaces that react to movement, touch or gestures. This makes active play and learning possible.
For which target groups is this suitable?
Our systems are suitable for children (3-12 years), adults and seniors. They are used in education, childcare, healthcare institutions, rehabilitation centers, arcades, events and sports facilities.
How difficult is the installation?
Our systems are plug-and-play. Everything is set up and ready to play within 15-30 minutes. No technical knowledge required. We offer professional installation service for permanent installations.
Can I rent the systems for an event?
Yes! Our Mobile Floor is specially designed for rental. We handle transport, setup and supervision. Perfect for company parties, festivals, open days and children's events.
How many games are available?
There are over 60 ready-to-play games available, ranging from educational exercises to active movement games. You can also create your own games with our Game Editor.
