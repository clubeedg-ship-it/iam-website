CREATE YOUR GAME

Create unique interactive games with the IAM Game Editor

The IAM game editor

With the IAM Game Editor, you create your own interactive games for our floor and wall projection systems. No programming skills required — choose a template, customize it with your own images, sounds and questions, and upload it directly to your InterActiveMove system. Within minutes, your own creation is ready to play!

How does it work?

Step 1

Choose a template

Select from dozens of game templates: quiz, memory, movement games, music games and more

Step 2

Customize

Add your own images, sound effects, questions and answers

Step 3

Upload

Upload your game to your InterActiveMove system via the online platform

Step 4

Play!

Your game is instantly playable on the interactive floor or wall

Use cases

education

Teachers create maths, language and geography games aligned with the curriculum. Students learn through movement.

healthcare & rehab

Therapists design custom exercises: balance training, cognitive stimulation and motor exercises as interactive games.

events

Create branded games for trade shows, product launches and corporate events. Your logo and brand identity in an interactive experience.
