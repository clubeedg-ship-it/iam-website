Interactive Wall

Wall projection for sports, education and entertainment

What is the Interactive Wall?

The Interactive Wall projects games and exercises onto any wall. Players touch targets, follow movements, or solve puzzles. Perfect for sports facilities, schools, rehabilitation centers, and entertainment venues.

Features

Sports Games

Active games that train coordination, speed, and reaction time.

Educational

Learn playfully with math, language, and memory exercises.

Any Wall

Works on virtually any flat wall, indoors or sheltered.

Rehabilitation

Therapeutic exercises for motor skills and cognition.
