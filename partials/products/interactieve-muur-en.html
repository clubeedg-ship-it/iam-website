Frequently Asked Questions

The system works on virtually any flat, light-colored wall. The ideal color is white or light gray. Light texture is acceptable. We recommend a minimum wall height of 2.5 meters and width of 3 meters for optimal play experience.

Depending on the projection size, 1 to 10 players can play simultaneously. Most games support 2-6 players for optimal interaction. Solo games are also available for individual training or therapy.

The standard system is designed for indoor use. For sheltered outdoor locations (covered terrace, tent) special solutions are possible. Contact us for the possibilities.

Our technicians install the complete system in 2-4 hours. This includes mounting the projector and camera, calibration, software installation and a brief training. We schedule installation at a time that suits you.

All software updates and new games are included free of charge. Updates are automatically installed when there is an internet connection. On average, we add 4-6 new games per year.
