Frequently Asked Questions about the 2-in-1

Can I project on the floor and wall simultaneously?
No, the system operates in one mode at a time — floor or wall. Switching takes just a few seconds via the software. Want both surfaces at once? Combine the 2-in-1 with a standalone floor or wall system.
How much space do I need?
For floor mode: at least 3.5 × 3 m of free floor space and a ceiling height of 2.7 – 3.5 m. For wall mode: a flat, light-coloured wall of at least 3 × 2.5 m. The system mounts to the ceiling and takes up no floor space.
What's the difference compared to two separate systems?
The 2-in-1 uses one projector, one sensor, and one computer for both modes. This saves ~40% on costs, halves the installation, and requires only one ceiling bracket and power outlet. The game libraries (floor and wall) are identical to the standalone systems.
How quickly can I switch between floor and wall?
Switching takes 5–10 seconds via the touchscreen or tablet app. No manual repositioning or recalibration needed — the software automatically adjusts the projection angle and sensor calibration.
Are the games the same as the standalone floor and wall?
Yes. The 2-in-1 system has the same game library as the standalone Interactive Floor and Interactive Wall. You don't miss out — you get both collections in one device.
Can I upgrade to two separate systems later?
Yes. If your needs grow, you can add a standalone floor or wall system at any time for simultaneous projection on both surfaces. Your existing 2-in-1 continues to function as normal.
