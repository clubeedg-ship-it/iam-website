Advanced Augmented Reality Technology

The Interactive Sandbox combines 3D depth sensors with real-time projection for a unique learning experience

3D Depth Sensing

Our advanced Kinect sensor scans the sand surface 30 times per second and creates an accurate 3D map. When children move the sand, the system instantly detects every elevation change and adjusts the projection in real-time.

  • 30 FPS scan rate for smooth response
  • Millimeter-precise elevation detection
  • Automatic calibration for perfect projection

Topography Visualization

The system automatically projects color-coded elevation maps onto the sand. Blue zones for valleys and water, green zones for lowlands, yellow and orange zones for hills, and white zones for snow-capped peaks. Perfect for geography lessons.

  • 8 elevation layers with realistic colors
  • Smooth color transitions
  • Educational contour lines optional

Water & Lava Simulation

Make a gesture above the sand to trigger water or lava flow. The system simulates realistic flow patterns that follow the landscape. Water collects in valleys and volcanoes erupt with lava effects when a mountain is tall enough.

  • Realistic water physics simulation
  • Automatic volcano detection
  • Hand gesture-controlled water addition

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Interactive Sandbox

For the standard sandbox (120x90cm) you need a minimum of 2.5 x 2 meters floor space, plus 2.5 meters ceiling height. The projector and sensor are mounted above the sandbox. We recommend some extra space around it for easy access for multiple users.

With normal use, the sand lasts for years. We use high-quality washed play sand that doesn't discolor or crumble. In environments with intensive use (like museums) we recommend refreshing the sand annually and raking it regularly to maintain optimal performance. Extra sand can be easily ordered.

The basic system comes with pre-installed game modes. Regular software updates with new games are free. For fully customized content or educational programs linked to your curriculum, we can develop custom solutions. Contact us for possibilities and pricing.

All packages come with 2-year full warranty (Enterprise: 3 years). For technical issues we first offer remote support via phone or TeamViewer. If necessary, we send a replacement part within 48 hours or visit for repairs. After the warranty period you can sign a maintenance contract.
