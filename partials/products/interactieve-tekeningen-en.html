Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with my existing climbing wall?
Yes! The interactive climbing wall is designed to work with virtually all existing climbing walls. The system projects onto the wall and detects touches on the holds. No modifications to the climbing wall itself are needed.
How does touch detection work?
We use advanced infrared sensors that can detect every touch of a climbing hold. The system recognizes in real-time which holds are being touched and adapts the game accordingly.
Is this safe for users?
Absolutely. The interactive climbing wall is completely contactless - it's a projection system that requires no physical modifications to the climbing wall. All standard climbing safety remains intact. The system is CE-certified.
Can I program my own routes?
Yes! With the Professional and Enterprise packages, you can create your own routes and games via our intuitive software. Ideal for climbing gyms that want to offer specific training routes.
How much space do I need?
The standard projection surface is 4x3 meters, suitable for an average climbing wall. We can also serve larger surfaces with multiple projectors. The projector is installed approximately 1.5 meters from the wall.
