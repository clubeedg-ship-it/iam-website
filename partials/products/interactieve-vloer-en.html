What is the Interactive Floor?

The Interactive Floor transforms any floor into a dynamic play surface. With advanced projection technology and motion sensors, the floor reacts immediately to every step, jump, or movement. Perfect for schools, arcades, healthcare facilities, and events.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the interactive floor?
The interactive floor is a projection system that projects images onto the ground that react to movement. With infrared sensors, we detect every step, jump, or touch, adapting the game in real-time.
How large is the projection area?
The standard projection area is approximately 3x2.5 meters. This offers enough space for 4-6 children to play simultaneously. Larger areas are possible with multiple systems.
How many games are available?
With our interactive floor, you get access to more than 60 ready-to-play games, ranging from educational exercises to active movement games. We regularly add new content via updates.
Is installation difficult?
No! The system is completely plug-and-play. Everything is connected and ready to play within 10-15 minutes. No special technical knowledge is required.
For what ages is it suitable?
The interactive floor is designed for children from 3 to 12 years old, but adults enjoy it too! There are games available for specific age groups and skill levels.
