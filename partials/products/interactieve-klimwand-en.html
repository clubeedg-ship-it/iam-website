Frequently Asked Questions

What climbing walls does the system work on?
The system works on virtually any flat, light-colored climbing wall. The ideal color is white or light gray. We recommend a minimum wall height of 2.5 meters and width of 3 meters for optimal play experience.
How many climbers can play at the same time?
Depending on the projection size, 1 to 10 climbers can play simultaneously. Most games support 2-6 players for optimal interaction. Solo games are also available for individual training or therapy.
How does installation work?
Our technicians install the complete system in 2-4 hours. This includes mounting the projector and camera, calibration, software installation and a brief training session. We schedule installation at a time that suits you.
Do I get new games after purchase?
All software updates and new games are included free of charge. Updates are automatically installed when there is an internet connection. On average, we add 4-6 new games per year.
Does the climbing wall work outdoors?
The standard system is designed for indoor use. For sheltered outdoor locations (covered terrace, tent) special solutions are possible. Contact us for the possibilities.
What ages is it suitable for?
The Interactive Climbing Wall is suitable for all ages and levels. There are games for specific age groups — from educational climbing games for children to rehabilitation exercises for adults and seniors.
