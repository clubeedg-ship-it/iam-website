Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us make the website function properly and improve it.

Which cookies do we use?

Necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Purpose Retention period
iam_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year
lang Remembers your language preference (NL/EN) 1 year

Analytical cookies

We currently do not use analytical cookies.

Should we add analytics in the future, we will ask for your consent first. These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

Marketing cookies

We do not use marketing cookies.

We do not place cookies for advertisements or tracking of your browsing behavior on other websites.

External services

To protect your privacy, we have taken the following measures:

  • Fonts: We host all fonts locally (no Google Fonts)
  • Scripts: HTMX is hosted locally (no external CDN)
  • Analytics: Currently not in use
  • Advertisements: We do not display advertisements

WhatsApp

When you click a WhatsApp link, you will be redirected to WhatsApp (owned by Meta). Meta may place cookies according to their own policy. We have no control over this. See the WhatsApp privacy policy for more information.

Managing cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie settings" button at the bottom of the page.

You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note: Disabling necessary cookies may limit the functionality of the website.

Changes

We may change this cookie policy. The most recent version is always available on this page with the date of the last modification.
